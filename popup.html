  • Create strong, easy-to-remember passwords
  • When creating any account, first type in your chosen password
  • Then click on the add toppings button to increase password security!
  • Toppings can be any combination of food items, where order matters
  • Your toppings will be encrypted and automatically added to the end of your password
  • When creating a password with toppings, you will need to add these topping each time you log in
  • These toppings are not case-sensitive, and we will never store your topping combination!
  • Your password with toppings can be saved normally by your browser
  • Like a normal password, if you forget your password or toppings, simply click "forgot password" to reset it!
